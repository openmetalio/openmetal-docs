How to Get Started with OpenStack

To get started using OpenStack, you will need to log in to Horizon, OpenStack's browser-based dashboard. Your administrator should provide you with the details you need to log in. If you do not have these details, reach out to your OpenStack administrator.

Once logged in, you are presented with the main dashboard of your OpenStack project. From here you can see a number of links on the left under the Project and Identity tabs as well as a summary of the cloud usage in the center. You will do most of your work out of the Project tab, where you can spin up instances, create networks and such. The Identity tab is where you will find user-based information, such as what project your user is associated with or details about your user.
